How to make Beaded dangle earrings

This project is a great place to start learning the art of jewlery making.
It isn't time consuming and it's simple so let's get right to it!

Jewelry and Tools you'll need.

  • Round-nose pliers
  • Wire cutters
  • Head pins or 20 gauge wire
  • A set of beads
  • Ear wire

If you want, you can make your own ear wire

STEP 1:


Take  the wire and put it through the briolettes bead hole.

Take the bead down about 3" on the wire.  It will leave about 10" of wire on one side and 3 inches on the other.


STEP 2:

Take both wires and bend upwards and criss-cross the wires making an X.

STEP 3:

Take your flat nose pliers and bend the short wire straight up.

This wire will be the stem and you are going to wrap the longer wire around the stem and top of the briolette bead.

STEP 4:

Start wrapping the top of the briolette bead with the longer wire.

As you are wrapping you can chose to keep your wrap clean and straight or more messy and care-free.

Keep wrapping until you have covered and passed the bead hole.

STEP 5:

Trim any remaining wire if needed. Use your flat nose pliers to tuck in the wire.

STEP 6:

Now its time to make the wrapped loop.

Take your round nose plies to the base of the stem.

Create a loop and wrap around the stem.

STEP 7:

Now attach to ear wire and repeat the process.
