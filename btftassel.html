How to make Beaded Leaf Dangle Earrings

This is the next step in making jewelry! With this walk through you'll learn how to add an accent bead.

You can use any style of accent you want. I am using metallic leafs as an accent to my carnelian bead.

Let's make something!

Jewelry making material list:

  • Two Fan Tassel Pendants(I bought these from Hobby Lobby for $5.99)
  • Two rough black tourmaline beads
  • Two red seed bead
  • Two 3 inch length of 20 gauge wire.
  • Two Ear-wires
  • Round nose pliers
  • Flat nose pliers
  • Wire cutters
  • Measuring tape

STEP 1:

Use one of your 3" piece of wire and create a headpin by adding a single wrapped loop.

Take your round nose pliers and grab the wire an inch down.

Bend the wire to create a loop and wrap the wire around the stem 3 times to create a single wrapped loop.

STEP 2:

Slide your red seed bead then black tourmaline onto your headpin.

Take your pliers to the base of the black tourmaline and make a open loop.

STEP 3:

Slide your Fan Tassel Pendant onto the loop.

Close the loop by bending the wire 3 times around the stem.

STEP 4:

Trim any excess wire.

Take your pliers and straighten your loops.

The loop that holds your ear wire should be a flat circle facing forward. The loop holding the pendant should be straight ended and sideways.

STEP 5:

Add Ear-wire. When holding your earring by the ear-wire, everything should be straight and facing the correct direction.
