How to make Beaded Leaf Dangle Earrings

This is the next step in making jewelry! With this walk through you'll learn how to add an accent bead.

You can use any style of accent you want. I am using metallic leafs as an accent to my carnelian bead.

Let's make something!

What you'll need.

  • 6 inches of 20-gauge wire
  • 2 ear wires
  • 2 leaf beads
  • 2 Accent beads
  • Round nose pliers
  • Flat nose pliers
  • Wire cutters
  • Measuring tape

STEP 1:
From your wire spool. Cut two, three inch lengths of wire.

STEP 2:

Take your round nose pliers an inch down and make a single wrapped loop. Use your wire cutters to remove the extra wire.

STEP 3:

Slide your accent bead onto your wire headpin.

STEP 4:

Take your round nose pliers and Make a single loop at the base of the bead. Keep it open to slide your leaf bead into the loop.

STEP 5:

Take your round nose pliers and clasp down onto the loop.

STEP 6:

Take your Flat nose pliers and grab the wire to wrap around the base 3 times, to create a wrapped loop. Cut remaining wire.

STEP 7:

Now add your ear wire and repeat!
